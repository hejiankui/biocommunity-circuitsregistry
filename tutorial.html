1.This is our homepage. You can choose to enter Mindroad or CircuitList by clicking the arrow button and clicking the corresponding enter button.


2.This is Mindroad. Click the bubble on the screen , several children bubble will popup .Then click any of these children bubbles you like , the corresponding children bubbles will popup.In this way, you can find a specific project.


3.Click the bacteria number, a tap will popup. In this tap ,you can get suffcient information about the project you chose.


4.Click the button at the bottom of the tap, you will enter the Logic Genetic Designer page.


5.In The LGD page, you can drag and drop any parts as you like. You can also delete parts or add parts, LGD can help calculate the relationship of every part of the new circuit.


6.Click the submit button in the navi, you will enter the submit page. fill out the table step by step , your project will be uploaded to our server. If passing our examnation, your project will be added to Mindroad and CircuitList.


7.CircuitList is a list of all the projects in our database. You can choose the sort order by clicking the sort button . You can also check the latest project by clicking the latest projects button. All the projects provided by our users will be show on the latest projects page.
